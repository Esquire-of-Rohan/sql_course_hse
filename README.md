# HSE 2025 | SQL for Data Analysis

The course is designed for students of the programm "Data Analysis for Goverment and Society"

- Lecture and seminar materials are stored in corresponding folders /Week n
- All communications for the course are held [here](https://t.me/+3BhrMB6k1Uc3NjM6)
- The textbook for you reference is [located](https://github.com/Esquire-of-Rohan/sql_course_hse/blob/main/SQL%20Queries%20for%20Mere%20Mortals%20Pearson%20UCertify%20Course%20Access%20--%20John%20L_%20Viescas%20--%20For%20Mere%20Mortals%2C%204%2C%202018%20--%20Addison-Wesley%20Professional%20--%209780134858333%20--%20d4bbc8ce47bf01b1db74daf5e4bc9a89%20--%20Anna%E2%80%99s%20Archive.pdf) within this repository

## Syllabus
- [Week 1](https://github.com/Esquire-of-Rohan/sql_course_hse/tree/main/Week1) | **Relational databases: DDL and DML**

- Week 2 | **DQL: SELECT, FROM, WHERE**

- Week 3 | **Advanced filtering and aggregate functions**

- Week 4 | **INNER JOIN, LEFT JOIN, RIGHT JOIN, FULL OUTER JOIN**

- Week 5 | **Subqueries and CTEs**

- Week 6 | **Window functions**

- Week 7 | **Introduction to BI and data visualizations**

- Week 8 | **Advanced BI**

## Database
Throughout the course we will be working with [Pagila database](https://github.com/devrimgunduz/pagila?tab=readme-ov-file). It represents a storage of various entities related to a DVD rental store.
You can find all the info about its structure [here](https://dev.mysql.com/doc/sakila/en/sakila-structure.html)
